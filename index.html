OSR MAP MAKER

Select/Move drags symbols, hexes and rivers/roads/borders (selected = gold); RHVI transform, Del removes. Draw River/Road/Border are freehand (drag); Border edge snaps to hex edges. Snap to grid aligns dungeon symbols. ¼-inch squares, export = 300 dpi. Esc cancels a stroke, Ctrl+Z undo. Zoom +− or Ctrl+wheel, 0 resets, Fit sizes to window.
