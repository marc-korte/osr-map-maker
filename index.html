OSR MAP MAKER

Select/Move drags symbols, names and rivers/roads/trails/borders (selected = gold); RHVI transform, Del removes. Drag the map itself to pan. Draw River/Road/Trail/Border are freehand (drag); Border edge snaps to hex edges. Snap to grid aligns dungeon symbols. ¼-inch squares, export = 300 dpi. Esc cancels a stroke, Ctrl+Z undo. Pan hold Space or drag empty space with Select/Move (middle-drag too). Zoom +− or Ctrl+wheel (pinch on touch), 0 resets, Fit sizes to window. On touch, drag = draw, two fingers = pan & zoom.
